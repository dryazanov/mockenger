In the world of RESTful APIs and web-services it is always a challenge to avoid dependencies between teams, projects and applications. Every day thousands of developers are struggling with impediments and just waiting, and waiting, and waiting... Very sad.

But you're on the right track to find the solution.

What is that? Mockenger is a mock-server that may help you get rid of dependencies and impediments. No need to wait when API is available. Start your development earlier, right after API specifications are defined.

Run Mockenger on your local machine or make it centralized and available for your team members and share your mocks with other teams.

Mockenger has sexy user-interface who said developers deserved only command-line tools?. Create mocks just with a couple of clicks. Or switch on recording and create mocks automatically! Cool, right? And this is just a small part of all the features. Excited?

No no no no no no. Mockenger is an open-source project, it's free.

 

So, a quick recap. Use Mockenger in case of:

  • You can't complete your tasks because of some unreliable jerks colleagues
  • There are strict rules and it takes ages to get changes in the API you are dependent on
  • You want to deliver your changes faster and not overcomplicate your project with new software containers or VMs
  • Performance tests run always a pain because of the problems with environments and/or test data

Mockenger won't solve all of your problems, but it definitely can help you with some of them.

Enjoy!
